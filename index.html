Show and Tell for Makers is a monthly meetup for technologists to share a recent small project. Instead of hacking something together in one caffeine-fueled weekend, we take our time and make something useful.

Some examples from prior events include:

  • A photo collage API
  • An arduino squid
  • A DIY cat scratching post made from found materials
  • A commit to a Safari plugin
  • A jQuery autocomplete plugin
  • A tutorial on jQuery SVG

We encourage makers to share their work online, whether by writing a blog post, creating a github repo, or scribbling a quick note in a txt file. All types of projects are welcome, and they do not have to be in any way coding related.
